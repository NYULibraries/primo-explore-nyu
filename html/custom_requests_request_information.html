Explaination of Delivery Services
Eligible borrowers may see one or more of the delivery options below, depending on the item type.
  • Request Item: Get the item delivered to the NYU library of your choice.
  • Request Scan: Request a chapter or article to be delivered to your NYU email.
  • Request E-ZBorrow: Get a book from one of our partner libraries in 3-6 days.
  • Request ILL: Request item, article, or chapter from another library.
